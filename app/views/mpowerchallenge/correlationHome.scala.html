PD Digital Biomarker DREAM Challenge: Correlation Networks

This site acts as a supplementary material for PD Digital Biomarker DREAM Challenge concluded in October 2018.

Here, you can freely freely explore the correlations among the submitted features at the high level. These are visualized as networks where nodes represent teams or submissions, and unidirectional edges reflect "similarities" between the nodes defined by aggregated absolute correlations (mean, max, and min).
